# Click ME 隐私政策

**生效日期**: 2025年11月  
**最后更新**: 2025年11月

---

## 1. 引言

欢迎使用 Click ME（以下简称"本应用"或"我们"）。我们非常重视您的隐私权，并致力于保护您的个人信息。本隐私政策说明了我们如何收集、使用、存储和保护您的信息。

**重要提示**：本应用**不收集、不存储、不传输任何个人身份信息或用户数据**。本隐私政策旨在向您说明我们如何处理（或不处理）您的数据，以及您的权利。

---

## 2. 信息收集

### 2.1 我们收集的信息

**我们明确声明：本应用不收集任何个人身份信息（PII）或用户数据。**

本应用设计为完全离线运行，不会：
- 收集您的姓名、电子邮件地址、电话号码或其他联系信息
- 收集您的位置信息
- 收集您的设备标识符（如 UDID、IDFA 等）
- 收集任何分析数据或使用统计
- 传输任何数据到我们的服务器或第三方服务器

### 2.2 本地存储的数据

本应用仅在您的设备上本地存储游戏相关数据，包括：
- 游戏分数和最高分记录
- 游戏时间和游戏次数
- 点击次数和命中率统计
- 等级进度和成就记录
- 游戏设置和偏好

**这些数据的特点**：
- ✅ 完全存储在您的设备本地
- ✅ 不会上传到任何服务器
- ✅ 不会与任何第三方共享
- ✅ 不会用于任何商业目的
- ✅ 您可以随时通过应用内的"清除历史记录"功能完全删除

---

## 3. 数据使用

由于我们不收集任何数据，因此不存在数据使用的情况。本地存储的游戏数据仅用于：
- 在您的设备上显示游戏统计和历史记录
- 提供游戏进度保存功能
- 改善您的游戏体验

---

## 4. 数据共享和披露

**我们不会与任何第三方共享、出售、出租或以其他方式披露您的任何信息**，因为：
1. 我们不收集任何数据
2. 所有数据仅存储在您的设备本地
3. 我们没有任何服务器来存储或处理数据

---

## 5. 第三方服务

### 5.1 Game Center

本应用使用 Apple 的 Game Center 服务来提供全球排行榜功能。当您使用 Game Center 功能时：

- Game Center 由 Apple Inc. 提供和管理
- 您的 Game Center 数据（如用户名、成就、排行榜排名）由 Apple 的隐私政策管理
- 我们无法访问或控制您的 Game Center 数据
- 您可以在设备的"设置" > "Game Center" 中管理您的 Game Center 账户和隐私设置

**有关 Game Center 的更多信息，请参阅**：[Apple 的隐私政策](https://www.apple.com/privacy/)

### 5.2 其他第三方服务

本应用**不使用**以下类型的第三方服务：
- ❌ 广告服务（如 Google AdMob、Facebook Audience Network 等）
- ❌ 分析服务（如 Google Analytics、Firebase Analytics 等）
- ❌ 崩溃报告服务（如 Crashlytics、Sentry 等）
- ❌ 用户行为追踪服务
- ❌ 任何数据收集或分析工具

---

## 6. 数据安全

虽然我们不收集或传输数据，但我们仍然重视您的隐私和安全：

- **本地数据加密**：游戏数据存储在 iOS 的安全沙盒环境中
- **无网络传输**：由于不收集数据，不存在数据在传输过程中被截获的风险
- **设备安全**：我们建议您使用设备密码、Face ID 或 Touch ID 来保护您的设备

---

## 7. 您的权利

根据适用的数据保护法律（如 GDPR、CCPA），您享有以下权利：

### 7.1 访问权
您可以随时通过应用内的"历史记录"功能查看所有本地存储的游戏数据。

### 7.2 删除权
您可以随时通过应用内的"清除历史记录"功能删除所有本地存储的游戏数据。删除后，数据将永久清除，无法恢复。

### 7.3 数据可移植性
由于数据仅存储在您的设备本地，您可以通过 iOS 的备份功能来备份或恢复您的游戏数据。

### 7.4 撤回同意
由于我们不收集数据，因此不需要您的同意。如果您不希望使用 Game Center 功能，可以在应用设置中禁用，或在设备设置中关闭 Game Center。

如果您是家长或监护人，并对我们如何处理儿童信息有任何疑问，请通过本政策末尾的联系方式与我们联系。

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Click ME (referred to as "this app" or "we"). We take your privacy seriously and are committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information.

**Important Notice**: This app **does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information or user data**. This privacy policy is intended to inform you about how we handle (or do not handle) your data and your rights.

---

## 2. Information Collection

### 2.1 Information We Collect

**We explicitly state: This app does not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) or user data.**

This app is designed to operate completely offline and does not:
- Collect your name, email address, phone number, or other contact information
- Collect your location information
- Collect your device identifiers (such as UDID, IDFA, etc.)
- Collect any analytics data or usage statistics
- Transmit any data to our servers or third-party servers

### 2.2 Locally Stored Data

This app only stores game-related data locally on your device, including:
- Game scores and high score records
- Game time and number of games played
- Click counts and hit rate statistics
- Level progress and achievement records
- Game settings and preferences

**Characteristics of this data**:
- ✅ Stored entirely on your device locally
- ✅ Not uploaded to any server
- ✅ Not shared with any third parties
- ✅ Not used for any commercial purposes
- ✅ Can be completely deleted at any time through the "Clear History" feature in the app

---

## 3. Data Usage

Since we do not collect any data, there is no data usage. Locally stored game data is only used to:
- Display game statistics and history on your device
- Provide game progress saving functionality
- Improve your gaming experience

---

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

**We do not share, sell, rent, or otherwise disclose any of your information to any third parties** because:
1. We do not collect any data
2. All data is stored only locally on your device
3. We do not have any servers to store or process data

---

## 5. Third-Party Services

### 5.1 Game Center

This app uses Apple's Game Center service to provide global leaderboard features. When you use Game Center features:

- Game Center is provided and managed by Apple Inc.
- Your Game Center data (such as username, achievements, leaderboard rankings) is governed by Apple's privacy policy
- We cannot access or control your Game Center data
- You can manage your Game Center account and privacy settings in your device's "Settings" > "Game Center"

**For more information about Game Center, please refer to**: [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/privacy/)

### 5.2 Other Third-Party Services

This app **does not use** the following types of third-party services:
- ❌ Advertising services (such as Google AdMob, Facebook Audience Network, etc.)
- ❌ Analytics services (such as Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, etc.)
- ❌ Crash reporting services (such as Crashlytics, Sentry, etc.)
- ❌ User behavior tracking services
- ❌ Any data collection or analytics tools

---

## 6. Data Security

Although we do not collect or transmit data, we still value your privacy and security:

- **Local Data Encryption**: Game data is stored in iOS's secure sandbox environment
- **No Network Transmission**: Since we don't collect data, there is no risk of data interception during transmission
- **Device Security**: We recommend using device passwords, Face ID, or Touch ID to protect your device

---

## 7. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws (such as GDPR, CCPA), you have the following rights:

### 7.1 Right of Access
You can view all locally stored game data at any time through the "History" feature in the app.

### 7.2 Right to Deletion
You can delete all locally stored game data at any time through the "Clear History" feature in the app. After deletion, the data will be permanently erased and cannot be recovered.

### 7.3 Data Portability
Since data is only stored locally on your device, you can backup or restore your game data through iOS's backup functionality.

### 7.4 Right to Withdraw Consent
Since we do not collect data, we do not require your consent. If you do not wish to use Game Center features, you can disable them in the app settings or turn off Game Center in your device settings.

- **COPPA Compliance**: Our practices comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
