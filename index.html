Click ME 隐私政策

最后更新日期: 2025年11月

数据收集

Click ME 不收集、存储或传输任何用户数据。

本地数据存储

本应用仅在您的设备上本地存储游戏记录（包括分数、游戏时间、点击次数等统计数据）。这些数据：

  • 仅存储在您的设备上
  • 不会上传到任何服务器
  • 不会与任何第三方共享
  • 您可以随时通过应用内的"清除历史记录"功能删除

第三方服务

本应用不使用任何第三方分析服务或广告服务。

网络访问

本应用仅在以下情况下访问网络：

  • Game Center 服务（用于全球排行榜功能，由 Apple 提供）
  • 这些网络访问由 Apple 的隐私政策管理

儿童隐私

本应用适合所有年龄段的用户，包括儿童。我们不收集任何个人信息，包括儿童的个人信息。

隐私政策变更

如果我们未来更新此隐私政策，我们会在应用内通知您，并更新此页面的"最后更新日期"。

联系我们

如果您对此隐私政策有任何疑问，请通过以下方式联系我们：

tap.me@icloud.com

本隐私政策自 2025年11月 起生效。

Click ME Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

Data Collection

Click ME does not collect, store, or transmit any user data.

Local Data Storage

This app only stores game records locally on your device (including scores, game time, click counts, and other statistics). This data:

  • Is stored only on your device
  • Is not uploaded to any server
  • Is not shared with any third parties
  • Can be deleted at any time through the "Clear History" feature in the app

Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party analytics or advertising services.

Network Access

This app only accesses the network in the following cases:

  • Game Center services (for global leaderboard features, provided by Apple)
  • These network accesses are governed by Apple's privacy policy

Children's Privacy

This app is suitable for users of all ages, including children. We do not collect any personal information, including children's personal information.

Privacy Policy Changes

If we update this privacy policy in the future, we will notify you within the app and update the "Last Updated" date on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at:

tap.me@icloud.com

This privacy policy is effective as of November 2025.
